Awesome birthday in Honolulu 🌺, our paper LoRA.rar won the Best Paper Award at the [P13N: Personalization in Generative AI Workshop](https://p13n-workshop.github.io/iccv2025/index.html) @ ICCV 2025! 🎉🦕

Huge thanks to the workshop organizers:  Pinar Yanardag, Rinon Gal, Daniel Cohen-Or, Tuna Han Salih Meral, Enis Simsar, Nupur Kumari, Ayşegül Dündar, Federico Tombari.

And perfect celebration at the [Krea X fal](https://partiful.com/e/ALwWWiVWKdtU5tmlgS9m) event 🥂, thanks [Krea](https://www.krea.ai/) for the cake and [fal](https://fal.ai/) for the hat 🥳.
